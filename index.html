About

Hi! My name is John Chen and I am a Research Engineer at Meta SuperIntelligence Labs on the Voice AI team, based out of New York City. My current research focuses on how we can make conversational AI systems more lifelike and engaging.

I completed my Master's and Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at University of Toronto, St. George Campus and Vector Institute under the supervision of Frank Rudzicz; my thesis "Exploring Text Specific and Blackbox Fairness Algorithms in Multimodal Clinical NLP" received Best Short Paper Award at ClinicalNLP 2020. During my time at U of T and Amazon Alexa, I published on a variety of topics in active learning, speech and fairness.

Outside of work, I am a runner and swimmer.

Talk to Plant

Architected and implemented the hardware system for an E2E house-plant IoT VoIP monitoring system. Talk to Plant allows you to call in from a mobile device to check on the status of your household plant, as monitored by various sensors hooked up to an ESP8266 Micro-controller.
